PhD Student

I am an aspiring cognitive neuroscientist. I am currently investigating how the brain constructs meaning from sound and how it represents different levels of linguistic processing when listening to naturalistic speech while its electrophysiological activity is recorded.

I am interested in prediction in the brain, research methodology, long inconclusive conversations about politics and society, and bikepacking.
